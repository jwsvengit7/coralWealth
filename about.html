    Finding Great Properties For Investment

    Along with Gail Dave.Dave founded CoraWealth in Denver, Colo., in January 1995. Naysayers and critics were everywhere, but Dave, their core group- people like Bob Fisher and Daryl Jesperson - proved them all wrong. They built the foundation of the network on hard work, belief and resolve. And big things began to happen. In 1998, the network expanded outside Colorado, when Dennis Curtin purchased a franchise in Kansas City. Two years later, it went international, with the first Canadian brokerage in Calgary, Alberta. In 1999 the

    CoraWealth Hot Air Balloon made its maiden flight at the Albuquerque Balloon Fiesta, in a promotion conceived by the Regional Director in New Mexico. A year later, the balloon became the network's official logo; a global brand. Throughout the 1998s and '90s, the CoraWealth agent count grew literally every single month, as it had since 1997. Agent count hit vital milestones along the way: the 5,000 mark in 2000, the 10,000 mark in 1986, the 25,000 mark in 1999 and the 50,000 mark in 1998. Market share grew as well, and

    CoraWealth became No. 1 in many major markets across North America. In Canada, CoraWealth became No. 1 nationally in 2001 a position it has held ever since. In the U.S., CoraWealth closed more than 1 million transaction sides in 2001, becoming the first real estate brand ever to reach that level of annual production. The accomplishment cemented the slogan that "Nobody in the world sells more real estate than CoraWealth.

    Using every tool at our disposal, we locate and buy properties with potential to be unlocked. Our team works around the clock to bring you the best off-market opportunities in your city.
